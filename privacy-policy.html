Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

At ProGameHub, we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our completely anonymous gaming platform.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Our games are designed to be played without registration, authentication, or any personal data collection.

The only information we may collect is anonymous, non-personal technical data that helps us improve our services:

  • Browser type and version (to optimize game performance)
  • Device type and screen resolution (to adjust game display)
  • Anonymous game statistics (scores, play time) for analytics

This data cannot be used to identify any individual user.

2. Cookies

We may use minimal cookies only to:

  • Remember game progress during your current session
  • Save basic game preferences (like sound on/off)

These cookies contain no personal information and are deleted when you close your browser.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use basic analytics services (like Google Analytics) to understand overall traffic patterns. These services only receive anonymous data.

4. Data Security

While we implement standard web security practices, please remember:

  • No personal data is collected, so none can be compromised
  • All games run directly in your browser with no downloads
  • No user accounts mean no risk of account breaches

5. Children's Privacy

Our games are suitable for all ages because:

  • No personal information is collected from anyone
  • No registration or age verification is required
  • All content is family-friendly

6. Changes to This Policy

If we ever make significant changes to our data practices, we will:

  • Post a clear notice on our website
  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
